How Does Population Change Affect Australia?

  • Due to the impacts of COVID-19 and the many policies cities adapted for public safety many resident left Australia especially from urban centers causing these unforseen changes in population across major cities.
  • This depopulaiton of cities also caused internal migration into region areas. Between 2016 and 2021, Australia's capital cities experienced a net loss of 160,100 people to regional areas.
  • The has caused major sectors such as Health and Education which makes up 12.8% of the economny to diminish as these industries rely number of workers and students.
  • To combat this in 2022 Australia changed its migration policy opening up Australia increasing placements by 35.8% increase compared to 2021 of which 73% were skill visas and 27% were family visas.
  • The effect of this has been a net gain in population across almost all regions of Australia in a very quick period of time causing increases in cost of living across essentials like housing, utilities and food.
